About Iqra Daycare & Preschool

Providing quality childcare and Islamic education in a nurturing environment.

Who We Are

At Iqra Daycare & Preschool, we are committed to offering exceptional childcare services that foster growth, learning, and development. Our facility is a safe and welcoming environment where children can explore, discover, and thrive.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the holistic development of each child. Our programs are designed to nurture not only the intellectual but also the social, emotional, and physical aspects of growth. We encourage curiosity and creativity, laying a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Flexibility: We understand the demands of modern life and offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your family's needs.
  • Quality Childcare: Our experienced staff is dedicated to providing the highest standard of care, ensuring your child's safety and happiness.
  • Islamic Education: For Muslim families, we offer Islamic educational programs, integrating cultural and religious teachings into daily activities.
  • Qaida Classes: Starting at age 4, children can begin learning Qaida, the foundational course for reading the Quran.

Our Staff

Our team consists of compassionate and qualified professionals who are passionate about early childhood education. They are committed to creating an inclusive environment where every child feels valued and supported.
