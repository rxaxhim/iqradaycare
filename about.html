About Iqra Daycare & Preschool

Providing quality childcare and Islamic education in a nurturing environment.

Who We Are

At Iqra Daycare & Preschool, located in Omaha, Nebraska, we are dedicated to providing exceptional daycare and childcare services that support children’s growth, learning, and development. Our home-based preschool offers a safe, nurturing environment where children can explore, discover, and thrive. Our programs emphasize holistic development, nurturing the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical aspects of each child’s growth. By encouraging curiosity and creativity, we help lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

State Licensing Requirements

Iqra Daycare & Preschool LLC is fully licensed to provide childcare services in Nebraska and adheres to all state licensing standards. These standards ensure that our daycare in Omaha meets rigorous requirements related to safety, nutrition, health procedures, caregiver-to-child ratios, and record-keeping. Our facility is subject to regular inspections by state and city health, fire, and licensing officials, all to guarantee a safe and supportive environment for your child.

Inclusion and Non-discrimination

At Iqra Daycare & Preschool, we believe that all children deserve equal opportunities for acceptance and participation in childcare. We make every reasonable accommodation to support full and active participation, based on each child’s individual needs and abilities. We offer equal educational opportunities for all children, regardless of race, color, creed, national origin, gender, age, ethnicity, religion, disability, or any other protected status. Our daycare’s programs are designed to meet the diverse needs of all children in Omaha’s vibrant community.

Our Staff

Our Omaha daycare team consists of compassionate, qualified professionals passionate about early childhood education. Our staff is dedicated to creating an inclusive preschool environment where every child feels valued and supported. We meet Nebraska’s requirements for licensed daycare providers, following all regulations for in-home daycares. Our childcare team is trained in child development, healthy and safe environments, developmentally appropriate practices, cultural and individual diversity, and professionalism to ensure your child receives high-quality care.
