Additional Information

We strive to be transparent with our pricing. Please note the following:

  • Registration Fee: A one-time non-refundable fee of $100 is due upon enrollment.
  • Sibling Discount: Receive a 10% discount on the second child's tuition.
  • Late Pickup Fee: A fee of $1 per minute is charged for pickups after 5:00 PM.
  • Payment Options: We accept cash, check, and major credit cards.
