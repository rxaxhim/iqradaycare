Our Mission

At Iqra Daycare & Preschool, our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive. We are committed to fostering holistic development by focusing on the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical growth of each child. Our dedicated staff works closely with families to ensure that every child receives personalized care and attention.

We also aim to instill strong moral values and cultural awareness. For our Muslim families, we offer Islamic educational programs, including basic teachings and Qaida classes starting at age 4. We embrace diversity and welcome children from all backgrounds, creating an inclusive community that celebrates differences and promotes understanding.
