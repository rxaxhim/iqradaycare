Our Mission

At Iqra Daycare & Preschool in Omaha, Nebraska, our mission is to provide high-quality childcare and early education where children can grow, learn, and thrive. We are committed to fostering holistic development, focusing on the intellectual, social, emotional, and physical growth of each child. Our dedicated staff works closely with families to ensure every child receives personalized care and attention, supporting their unique needs.

In addition to our focus on academic and social development, we emphasize strong moral values and cultural awareness. For Muslim families, we offer Islamic educational programs. We welcome children from all backgrounds, celebrating diversity and creating an inclusive daycare community that promotes understanding and respect.
